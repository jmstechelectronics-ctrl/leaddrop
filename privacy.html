Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What we collect

We collect the information you provide when signing up: your name, business name, email address, phone number, and the service categories you select. We also collect your suburb to match you with local leads.

2. How we use it

Your information is used solely to deliver the LeadDrop service — matching you with relevant local leads, sending alerts via Telegram, email, or SMS, and processing your subscription payment via Stripe.

3. Payment information

We do not store your credit card details. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used exclusively within LeadDrop to provide the service you signed up for.

5. Communications

We send lead alerts via your chosen delivery method (Telegram, email, or SMS). We may also send occasional service updates. You can cancel your subscription at any time.

6. Data retention

We retain your account information as long as your subscription is active. Upon cancellation, your data is deleted within 30 days.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at info@lead-drop.com.au.
